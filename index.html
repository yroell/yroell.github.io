Graduate Student

Parent Lab - University of Idaho

My proposed work currently is working on the link between morphological and physiological changes with environmental variation which would allow for the understanding of why an adaptation would arise and why a lineage has diversified. My research so far has been looking at the relationship of metabolic rate to shell morphology and environmental differences in endemic land snails of Galapagos.
